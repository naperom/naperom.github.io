Authenticity, the word that best defines Tiago's work. He always has the same starting point:
the singularity of the human being. Everyone has a different story to tell... both are exploring and looking for answers and, in the end, both are telling their story in deep connection with themselves.
Tiago shapes his work to the uniqueness of someone's sexuality. Every video and photo reflect pure human nature: it's sexual intimacy, behaviours, desires, in an authentic, inclusive and respectful way.
