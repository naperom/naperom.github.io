My focus is on self love, self empowerment and what is real. In movement, emotions and sensations. In each session, I propose to create a safe physical and emotional space to give each person total freedom to express themselves as they want, feel or imagine, using my lens as an extension to register an intimate moment where I am in absolute presence, open to receive vulnerability and trust. We are all sexual and sensual beings, each in it’s own way and that is what I want you to see and feel. I want to break paradigms and claim my idea that creating visual art with a naked body is natural, true and limitless.
